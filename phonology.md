* Consonants become voiced under certain conditions.
* e varies from /e/ to /e/gg to /i/gloo.
* y varies from schwa to /v/nder.

# Conventions
' for glottal stop
y for schwa
V for any of {a, e, i, o, u, y}
W for any of {a, e, o, u, y}

# Phonological Constraints
(s)(p,t,k,f,n,')(j)V
